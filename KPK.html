Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, often abbreviated as KPK, is located in the northwestern region of Pakistan, known for its rugged mountains, rich cultural heritage, and hospitality of its people.

Major Cities

Peshawar

Peshawar, the capital city of KPK, is one of the oldest cities in South Asia, known for its historical landmarks, bustling markets, and vibrant culture.

Swat

Swat Valley is a picturesque region in KPK, famous for its lush green hills, crystal-clear rivers, and archaeological sites dating back to Buddhist era.

Education

University of Peshawar

The University of Peshawar is one of the oldest universities in Pakistan, offering a wide range of academic programs and research opportunities.

Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan

Located in Mardan, this university is renowned for its quality education and contribution to higher education in KPK.
